# Experiments & Insights

## Experiment 1: Decadal Shift Analysis
Hypothesis: Climate indicators accelerate in 2011-2023 vs 2000-2010
- Temperature Δ: ±0.06°C (~0.4%)
- CO2 Δ: ±0.5 ppm (~0.12%)
- Precipitation volatility decreased

## Experiment 2: Acceleration & Tipping Points
- Slope 2000-2011: -0.0256 °C/year
- Slope 2011-2023: 0.00222 °C/year
- Acceleration factor: -0.09x
- Insight: Warming is non-linear

## Experiment 3: Climate Sensitivity Logic
- Temp rise per 1 ppm CO2: -0.01601 °C
- Interpretation: 10 ppm CO2 increase → ~0.16°C transient response (synthetic)

## Experiment 4: Statistical Outliers & Anomalies
- No extreme temperature, precipitation, or wind events detected
- Dataset highly linear and consistent
